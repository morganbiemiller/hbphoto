Meet Haley

Haley has always been one for spontaneity and living in the present. In 2011 she started collecting old digital and Polaroid cameras as a hobby to start capturing her adventures. This sparked a passion for photography that led to a lot of experimenting until she got her first professional camera and began capturing other people’s special moments in 2012. Haley has experience photographing all kinds of occasions and people. When she’s not in class or shooting you can find her on a road trip somewhere in America, at a concert chillin’ with Katy Perry’s dog, in a Jeep off-roading, perhaps at a drag show… good luck.
